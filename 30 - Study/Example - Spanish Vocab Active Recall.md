# 🗣️ Active Recall: Spanish Food Vocab

> Example deck. The trick is simple: read the prompt, say the answer out loud, THEN scroll to check. If you miss it, mark it and redo it tomorrow. Delete when you get it.

**Class:** [[Spanish MOC]]

## How to run this

1. Cover the answers with your hand or fold below the line.
2. Read each prompt. Say the answer out loud.
3. Reveal. Right, move on. Wrong, put a dot next to it.
4. Next session, only redo the dotted ones.

---

**Q:** the apple
**Q:** to eat
**Q:** the restaurant
**Q:** I would like (to order politely)
**Q:** the bill / check

---
Answers (do not peek until you have tried):

- the apple → la manzana
- to eat → comer
- the restaurant → el restaurante
- I would like → quisiera
- the bill / check → la cuenta
