# 📕 The Great Gatsby

> Example book note. Use the Book Note template. Delete when you get it.

**Author:** F. Scott Fitzgerald
**Read for:** [[English MOC]]

## What it is about

A poor man reinvents himself as a rich one to win back a woman he loved years ago, and it destroys him. Under that, it is about the gap between the American Dream people are sold and what they actually get.

## Ideas and quotes that stuck

- The green light: wanting something so badly that the wanting matters more than the thing.
- "So we beat on, boats against the current." The whole book in one line.
- Money does not buy the past back.

## What I think

The characters are hard to like, which I think is the point. Fitzgerald is not asking you to root for them, he is showing you what chasing status does to people. Reminds me of how social media works now.
