## How to take a book note that is worth keeping

You do not need to summarize the whole thing. Capture three things:

1. What is it actually about, in one or two sentences.
2. Two or three ideas or quotes that stuck with you.
3. What you think about it. Agree, disagree, reminds you of what.

The third one is where your thinking lives. That is the part AI cannot do for you.
