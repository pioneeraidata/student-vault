# 🔬 Science

The home page for this class. Science splits into two note types: **concepts** (how something works and why) and **labs** (what you did, what you saw, what it means).

## Current unit

> Example: Cell biology, then genetics.

## Class notes

> Concept notes explain a mechanism in your own words. If you cannot explain it simply, you do not understand it yet. That is the signal to ask a question.

## Labs

> Use the Assignment template for lab reports. Capture: the question, what you did, your data, and what it means. Link them here.
