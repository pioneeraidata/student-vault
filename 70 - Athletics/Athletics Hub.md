# 🏅 Athletics Hub

The home for your sport. Practices, games, goals, and the honest reflection that actually makes you better. Coaches notice the players who work on the right things, not just the ones who show up.

This is also where a big chunk of your future lives. Sports are one of the strongest things you can put on a [college application](../60%20-%20College%20%26%20Future/College%20Prep%20Hub.md), and if you might play in college, the record you keep here becomes your recruiting profile. Keep it current and future you cashes it in.

## The habit that separates players

After a game or a hard practice, take two minutes. What went well, what to fix, what the coach said. See [[Example - Game Log - vs Central]]. Most players never do this, so most players make the same mistake for three years. You will not.

## In season = time gets tight

When your sport is in season, your free time drops hard. That is exactly when school, sleep, and everything else start to collide. Do not wing it. Read [Managing Your Time](../90%20-%20System/Guides/Managing%20Your%20Time.md) and put your practice and game days into [[My Commitments]] so the rest of your week plans around them.
