# 🎵 Music Hub

The home for your music. Lessons, practice, the pieces you are working, and every performance and audition. Whether it is band, orchestra, choir, or your own guitar in the garage, this keeps the thread so you can see yourself getting better over months, not just days.

Like sports, music is real weight on a [college application](../60%20-%20College%20%26%20Future/College%20Prep%20Hub.md), and chair auditions and recitals come with hard deadlines that collide with test weeks. Keep the dates here and in [[My Commitments]] so nothing sneaks up.

## Practice, the smart way

Practice is not "play it start to finish five times." It is finding the two bars you keep missing and drilling those slowly until they are boring. Log the hard spot in the piece note, slow it down, speed it back up. Same idea as [active recall](../90%20-%20System/Guides/Study%20Smarter.md) for studying: attack the part you cannot do, not the part you can.
