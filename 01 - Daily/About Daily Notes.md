# 📅 About Daily Notes

The daily note is the single most important habit in this vault. If you do nothing else, do this.

Each school day, open today's note. There is a button in the left sidebar (calendar icon) and a command called "Open today's daily note." Give it a hotkey (see [Setup](../90%20-%20System/Guides/Setup%20-%20Turn%20On%20These%20Plugins.md)).

## Why one note per day works

- It is a **timeline of your life and school year**. Six months from now you can scroll back and see exactly what you were working on.
- It is a **capture point**. You do not have to decide where something goes. Write it in today's note, link out later if it matters.
- It **connects everything**. When you type `[[` and the name of a class or assignment, this note joins the web. Later, that class page shows every day you touched it.

## What goes in it

The template gives you a start:

- The one thing that matters most today
- Homework and to-dos
- Notes and thoughts as the day happens
- A two minute end of day review

You do not have to fill every box. Empty boxes are not failure. Showing up is the point.

## The move that makes it powerful

When you write about a class, wrap the class name in double brackets: `[[English]]`. When you mention an assignment, do the same: `[[Gatsby Essay]]`. Those links turn a pile of daily notes into a searchable, connected memory. That is the difference between a diary and a second brain.
