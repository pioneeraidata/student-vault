# 🎒 Start Here

Welcome. This is your brain, in plain text, that you own forever. Nothing here is trapped in someone else's app. Everything links to everything.

If you only remember one thing: **open your daily note, dump your day into it, and link out from there.** The rest of this vault is just nicer homes for the stuff you dump.

---

## ▶️ The one habit

Every school day, open today's **Daily Note** (calendar icon in the left sidebar, or the "Open today's daily note" command). Write in it. That is the whole system.

The loop this vault is built around:

> **Capture. Keep. Understand.**
> Get the thought out fast. Keep what matters. Then question it until you actually understand it.

Read [The Loop](90%20-%20System/Guides/The%20Loop%20-%20Capture%20Keep%20Understand.md) once. It explains the three tools (voice to text, this vault, and an AI that only answers from your own sources).

## 🚀 First 15 minutes (do this once)

1. Read [How to Use This Vault](90%20-%20System/Guides/How%20to%20Use%20This%20Vault.md). It is short.
2. Turn on the two settings that make this vault work: [Setup - Turn On These Plugins](90%20-%20System/Guides/Setup%20-%20Turn%20On%20These%20Plugins.md).
3. Open the [Classes MOC](10%20-%20Classes/Classes%20MOC.md) and rename the example subjects to your real classes.
4. Open today's daily note and write two sentences about your day. That is it. You started.
