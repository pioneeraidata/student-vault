# 🏷️ Tags & Status

Light conventions so search works and nothing gets lost. Do not overthink these. Folders and links do most of the work. Tags are a bonus.

## Status (on assignments and projects)

In the properties at the top of an assignment note, use one of:

- `todo`: not started
- `doing`: in progress
- `done`: finished

That is the whole system. Three words. It lets you scan the [Assignments Dashboard](../../20%20-%20Assignments/Assignments%20Dashboard.md) and see what is live.

## Tags

Tags are words with a `#` that group notes across folders. The templates already add useful ones. The main family:

- `#class/english`, `#class/math`, and so on: one per subject
- `#assignment`, `#study`, `#reading`, `#project`, `#college`

Click the tag pane in the left sidebar to see them all. Click a tag to see every note that has it. Example: `#study` pulls up every study guide and recall deck across all classes at once.

## The rule

If you are ever unsure whether to tag something, do not. A missing tag costs nothing, you can still find the note by searching or by its links. Tags are a nice-to-have, not a chore. The daily note and links are the system. Tags just make the search a little sharper.
