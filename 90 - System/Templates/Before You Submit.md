# Before You Submit: {{title}}

The last gate. Never turn something in straight off the last sentence you wrote. Put your reviewer hat on and run this first. See [[Be Your Own Reviewer]].

**Assignment:** [[ ]]

## Did it clear the bar?

- [ ] It answers the question that was actually asked (not the one I wish was asked)
- [ ] It hits every point in the rubric I wrote at the start (see [[Do Work You're Proud Of]])
- [ ] Every claim has evidence behind it, no missing steps
- [ ] Any fact I could not source is checked or cut
- [ ] I read it fresh, out loud or the next morning, not right after writing it
- [ ] If I used AI, the work is mine and reads like me (see [[Ask AI the Right Way]])
- [ ] Name, formatting, and the boring stuff are right
- [ ] It is turned in to the right place, the right way
