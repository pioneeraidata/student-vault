## Study your lowest scores first

## The answer skeleton

> For essays, labs, and short-answer: Finding, then Evidence, then Why it matters, then What it means. Use this shape and you never freeze on a blank page.

**Finding:** what is true / what you observed
**Evidence:** the proof from the source or the data
**Why it matters:** so what
**Conclusion:** what to do or think about it
