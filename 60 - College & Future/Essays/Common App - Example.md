# 📝 Common App Essay (Example)

> Example. Use the College Essay template for each essay. Draft ugly, revise later. A bad first draft you can fix beats a blank page you cannot. Delete when you get it.

**Prompt:** Share a story that reveals something essential about who you are.
**Word limit:** 650
**Status:** drafting

## The story I am telling

> One real moment, told in detail. Not your whole life. One scene that shows who you are.

## Why this one

> What does this moment reveal about you that a transcript cannot?

## Draft

> Write it here. Talk it out loud first if the page is blank. See [The Loop](../../90%20-%20System/Guides/The%20Loop%20-%20Capture%20Keep%20Understand.md).

## Revision notes

- [ ] Does the first sentence make someone want to keep reading?
- [ ] Is it specific and true, or impressive and vague?
- [ ] Read it out loud. Cut anything that sounds like a college essay.
- [ ] Would only I could have written this? If a classmate could swap their name in, dig deeper.
