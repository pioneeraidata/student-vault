# 🏫 College List

Schools you are curious about. No commitment, just research you will be glad you saved. Add a row any time one catches your eye. Sort into reach / target / likely once you have test scores.

## The list

| School | Location | Why it is on here | Type | Deadline |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: University of Michigan | Ann Arbor, MI | strong engineering, good vibe on the visit | target | |
| | | | | |

Type = reach (a stretch), target (a good match), or likely (very likely in). A balanced list has a few of each.

## For each school worth a closer look, note

- What it is known for
- Rough cost and what aid looks like
- What they want in an application
- Anything from a visit or a person you talked to
