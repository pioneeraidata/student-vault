# 🎓 College & Future Hub

You do not need to stress about this as a freshman. But a few notes kept along the way turn senior year from a panic into a checklist. This is the folder future you unlocks.

## Pages

- [[College List]]: schools you are curious about, with the facts that matter
- [[Activities Resume]]: your one page brag sheet, built from [[Activities & Clubs]]
- [[Test Scores & Deadlines]]: SAT / ACT / AP scores and every application date
- [Essays](Essays/Common%20App%20-%20Example.md): where application essays live and get revised

## When to care (rough guide)

- **Freshman and sophomore:** just keep [[Activities & Clubs]] current. That is 90 percent of the work, spread thin.
- **Junior year:** build the [[College List]], take the SAT or ACT, log scores in [[Test Scores & Deadlines]].
- **Senior year:** essays and applications. If the folders above are filled in, this is assembly, not archaeology.

## The essay secret

The best college essays are specific and true, not impressive and vague. The raw material is already in this vault: your [daily notes](../01%20-%20Daily/About%20Daily%20Notes.md), your [[Ideas]], the real moments from your [[Activities & Clubs]]. You will not have to invent a story. You will have three years of them.
