## Someday (the big stuff)

> No pressure and no deadline. Just what you want your life to look like. Come back to it a few times a year.

## How to use this

Each goal should have a next step small enough to do this week. "Get better at math" is a wish. "Redo every problem I missed on the last quiz" is a step. Put the step in your daily note.
