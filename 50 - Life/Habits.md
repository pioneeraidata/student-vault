# 🔁 Habits

Goals get hit by habits, not by motivation. Motivation runs out by Tuesday. A habit you barely notice keeps going.

Pick one or two. Not ten. A habit you actually keep beats a perfect list you abandon in a week.

## What I am building

| Habit | Why | Cue (when do I do it?) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Example: 5 min of Spanish | quiz every Friday | right after I sit down at my desk |
| | | |

## Track it in the daily note

Do not build a fancy tracker. Just tick the box in your [daily note](../01%20-%20Daily/About%20Daily%20Notes.md). At the [Weekly Review](../90%20-%20System/Templates/Weekly%20Review.md), glance back and see how many days you actually did it. That is all the tracking you need.

## The rule that makes habits stick

Never miss twice. Missing one day is life. Missing two is the start of quitting. If you skip, just do not skip again the next day.
