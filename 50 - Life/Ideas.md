# 💡 Ideas

The parking lot. Anything you might want to make, write, start, learn, or try. Most ideas go nowhere and that is fine. The point is to catch the good one when it shows up, because you will not remember it later.

## How to work this

When an idea keeps coming back and you cannot stop thinking about it, that is the signal to move it into [Projects territory](../60%20-%20College%20%26%20Future/College%20Prep%20Hub.md) or just start it. The rest can sit here forever with no guilt.

> Voice capture is perfect for this. Talk the idea out loud into your daily note the second it hits, then move it here later. See [The Loop](../90%20-%20System/Guides/The%20Loop%20-%20Capture%20Keep%20Understand.md).
