# 🌱 Life Hub

School is not the whole story. This is where the rest of you lives: what you want, what you are into, the teams and clubs, and the random ideas that might turn into something. Colleges and future you both care about this part more than you would guess.

## Pages

- [[Goals]]: what you are working toward, this year and beyond
- [[Activities & Clubs]]: sports, clubs, jobs, volunteering, with dates and roles
- [[Ideas]]: the parking lot for anything you might want to build, write, or try
- [[Habits]]: the small daily things you are trying to keep

## Why bother tracking this

Two reasons. One, writing down a goal makes it about ten times more likely to happen, because you actually see it. Two, when it is time to apply to colleges or jobs, [[Activities & Clubs]] becomes your resume in about five minutes instead of a panicked night of trying to remember what you did sophomore year.
